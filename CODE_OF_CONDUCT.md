# Code Of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this project to be a welcoming, respectful, and productive space for students, organizers, alumni, partners, and contributors.

Everyone participating in this project is expected to act with respect, assume good intent, and help keep discussions focused on improving the community website and related public resources.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Give and receive feedback professionally.
- Keep discussions relevant to the project.
- Credit people and sources when appropriate.
- Respect privacy and consent, especially for student information and event media.
- Help newcomers understand the contribution process.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discrimination based on identity, background, religion, gender, disability, age, nationality, or experience level.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Sexualized, violent, or otherwise inappropriate content.
- Spam, trolling, or repeated off-topic behavior.
- Misrepresenting affiliation with AWS, FAST NUCES, partners, or community organizers.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject pull requests, or block contributors when behavior harms the project or community.

If you see behavior that violates this code, contact the maintainers through the project's public GitHub channels or the official community contact route listed on the website.

Reports should include:

- What happened
- Where it happened
- Who was involved
- Any relevant links or screenshots

Maintainers should handle reports respectfully and avoid sharing private details beyond what is needed to resolve the issue.
